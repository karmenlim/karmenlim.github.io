Unraveling the truth

Doctor/Barre/200RYT/dancer in Melbourne that knows no limit,
because beyond the sky there's a whole new universe.

Karmen has been trained in ballet since the tender age of 5 and did various dance styles including Hip Hop, Jazz and Latin. After relocating to Melbourne from Malaysia, she continues to train and expand her passion for fitness and health. Now, she is a certified medical doctor (MD), a yoga teacher, a barre teacher and a personal trainer.

Combining the knowledge of western medicine with her current fitness, barre and yoga practice, Karmen is determined to share her journey to a blissful and happy life with her clients. Her class is challenging, synchronises breath, movement and mind, inspiring everyone to honour their practices and find balance in life.
