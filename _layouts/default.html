Unraveling the truth

Doctor/Barre/200RYT/dancer in Melbourne that knows no limit,
because beyond the sky there's a whole new universe.

Karmen has been trained in ballet since the tender age of 5 and did various dance styles including Hip Hop, Jazz and Latin. She continues to train and expand her passion for fitness and health. Now, she is a certified medical doctor (MD), a yoga teacher, a barre teacher and a personal trainer.

Through her years of studying medical and working in the health sector, Karmen realises that as she strive for her dreams of educating and treating patients, it was just not enough. Patients are often confused between being treated for a medical condition and being healthy. Why would a man end up with a diabetic foot and had to have it amputated to stop the flesh-eating bacteria? It all comes down to education of a healthy lifestyle. In other words, how would you prevent a fire? And not having the firefighter to come around for a preventable emergency. Although the saying has been around for ages, it has never been clearer to her that 'Prevention is always better than cure'.

Combining the knowledge of western medicine with her current fitness, barre and yoga practice, Karmen is determined to share her journey to a blissful and happy life with her clients. Teaching to inspire and encourage everyone to a healthy body and beautiful mind.
